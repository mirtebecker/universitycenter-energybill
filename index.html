Paying for electricity at the New School?

This is a prototype that we (Fei Liu and Mirte Becker) made for a class called Collab: University Center. Students pay for the utilities of the university buildings. We're looking into personalizing all this data and creating a system of utility credits, so to create a feedback loop for energy usage at the New School for the people that pay for it, and to give you a frame of reference of what electricity in the city costs.
